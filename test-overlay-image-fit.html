Overlay Image Fit Options Test

Test Results

This test verifies that the overlay image fit options are working correctly:
  • Fill: Should stretch the image to fill the entire area
  • Cover: Should maintain aspect ratio and cover the entire area (may crop)
  • Contain: Should maintain aspect ratio and fit inside the area (no overflow)
Test Status: Ready to test

How to Test

1. Open the chart builder application
2. Go to the "Overlay" panel
3. Add an image overlay
4. Try the different "Image Fit" options (Fill, Cover, Contain)
5. Verify that each option behaves as expected

Expected Behavior

Fill: Image stretches to completely fill the container, potentially distorting the aspect ratio
Cover: Image scales to cover the entire container while maintaining aspect ratio, may crop parts of the image
Contain: Image scales to fit entirely within the container while maintaining aspect ratio, may leave empty space
